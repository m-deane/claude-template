# AI Drone Video Reel Creation Prompt

Use this prompt with AI video editing tools or as a guide for your workflow.

---

## Universal Prompt for AI Video Tools

```
Create a professional Instagram Reel from my drone footage with the following specifications:

VIDEO STYLE:
- Cinematic travel/adventure aesthetic
- Dynamic pacing with varied shot lengths
- Smooth transitions that match the energy of the footage
- Professional color grading with enhanced contrast and vibrant colors

STRUCTURE:
- Duration: 30-60 seconds
- Format: 9:16 vertical (Instagram Reels optimized)
- Resolution: 4K or highest available

EDITING REQUIREMENTS:
1. Select the most visually striking moments from each clip
2. Arrange clips to create a sense of journey/progression
3. Sync all cuts and transitions to the beat of the music
4. Apply stabilization to any shaky footage
5. Use cinematic transitions (smooth wipes, zoom transitions, light leaks)
6. Color grade for a cohesive, professional look

MUSIC:
- [Specify: Upbeat/Dramatic/Chill/Ambient]
- Tempo: [Specify BPM or mood - e.g., "energetic 120+ BPM" or "relaxed cinematic"]
- Genre: [Specify: Electronic/Orchestral/Acoustic/Lo-fi]

PACING:
- Open with a strong hook shot (first 1-3 seconds)
- Build energy through the middle section
- Include at least one dramatic reveal or perspective shift
- End with a memorable closing shot

OUTPUT:
- Export at highest quality for Instagram
- Include optional version with captions/location text
```

1. Import all drone clips
2. Select "Auto Reframe" for 9:16
3. Use "Beat Sync" feature:
   - Select music track
   - Choose "High Energy" or "Cinematic" template
   - Let AI auto-cut to beats
4. Apply "Film" or "Cinematic" color filter
5. Review and adjust hero moments
6. Export at 4K 60fps

## DIY Workflow Prompt (Manual + AI Hybrid)

### Step 1: Preparation
```
Organize footage into folders:
/hero-shots (best 5-10 clips)
/establishing (wide scenic views)
/action (movement, reveals, flyovers)
/details (close-ups, interesting angles)
```

### Step 2: AI Processing
```
Run each clip through:
1. Topaz Video AI - Stabilization + Enhancement
2. Import to editing software
3. Apply AI color match to first clip (set the look)
4. Use AI to extend color grade to all clips
```

### Step 3: Assembly
```
Timeline structure (45-second example):
0:00-0:03 - Hook (most dramatic shot)
0:03-0:08 - Establishing context (2 wide shots)
0:08-0:25 - Build sequence (6-8 varied shots, increasing pace)
0:25-0:35 - Peak energy (fastest cuts, most dynamic footage)
0:35-0:42 - Resolution (slowing pace, 2-3 beautiful shots)
0:42-0:45 - Closing (memorable final frame, hold 3 seconds)
```

### Step 4: Polish
```
Final AI passes:
- Beat sync check (ensure cuts land on beats)
- Audio ducking (if using multiple audio sources)
- Export optimization for Instagram (H.264, 30fps, high bitrate)
```

## Pro Tips

1. **First 3 seconds matter most** - Use your most stunning shot as the hook
2. **Shoot more than needed** - AI needs variety to select the best moments
3. **Vary your shots** - Mix wide, medium, and detailed perspectives
4. **Include movement** - AI responds well to dynamic footage with clear motion
5. **Consistent lighting** - Footage from similar times of day grades better together
6. **Golden hour footage** - Dramatic lighting makes AI color grading more effective
