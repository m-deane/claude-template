This breakdown surprised our team. We spent weeks optimizing the model itself — trying quantization, ONNX runtime, batch inference — only to realize that model inference is just 18% of total latency. The real bottleneck is feature extraction at 35%, where we're computing rolling aggregates over the last 30 days of transaction history. That's where we're focusing our optimization efforts now. Caching frequently accessed features cut this segment by 40% in our latest tests.
