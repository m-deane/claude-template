### 2. Hook-First Selection
- **Opening 3 seconds rule**: Always select highest-scoring hook segment first
- **Subject density scoring**: More visible subjects = higher score
- **Action detection**: Prioritize moments with splashes, wakes, movement

### 3. Color/Lighting Awareness
- **Golden hour detection**: Boost scores for warm color temperatures
- **HLG/Log detection**: Flag clips needing color grading
- **Exposure balance**: Prefer mid-range histograms

### 4. Composition Scoring

- **Rule of thirds subjects**: Score based on subject positioning
- **Depth layers**: Prefer frames with foreground/background separation
